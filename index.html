Mathematics Syllabus

Below is a comprehensive overview of the mathematics syllabus from 10th grade to MSc level:

  • 10th Grade: Basic Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Probability, Statistics.
  • 12th Grade: Advanced Algebra, Calculus, Vectors, Matrices, Differential Equations.
  • BSc Mathematics: Linear Algebra, Real Analysis, Abstract Algebra, Complex Analysis, Differential Geometry, Numerical Methods.
  • MSc Mathematics: Advanced Calculus, Topology, Functional Analysis, Number Theory, Partial Differential Equations, Mathematical Modelling.
